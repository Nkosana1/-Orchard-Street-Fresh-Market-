Our Story

Orchard Street Fresh Market is a vibrant, family-owned fruit and vegetable market in the heart of NYC's Lower East Side. For 30 years, we've been serving our community with the freshest, locally-sourced and exotic produce.

It all started in 1995 when the Martinez family opened a small produce stand on Orchard Street. What began as a dream to bring farm-fresh quality to the neighborhood has grown into a beloved institution, but we've never lost sight of our roots.

Every morning before dawn, we personally select the finest produce from local farms in New York, New Jersey, and Pennsylvania. Our commitment to quality and authenticity has made us a neighborhood favorite. We know our customers by name, remember their preferences, and take pride in helping them discover new flavors. Whether you're a professional chef, a home cook, or someone just looking for the perfect apple, we're here to help you find exactly what you need.

After three decades, we're still family-owned and operated, and we're still passionate about bringing the best of the farm to your table. Thank you for being part of our story.
