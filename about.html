Our Story

Orchard Street Fresh Market is a vibrant, family-owned fruit and vegetable market in the heart of NYC's Lower East Side. For generations, we've been serving our community with the freshest, locally-sourced and exotic produce.

Our commitment to quality and authenticity has made us a neighborhood favorite. We work directly with local farmers and trusted suppliers to bring you the best selection of fruits and vegetables year-round.
